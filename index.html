About Me

Hey there! My name is Roshan Munjal, and I'll be going into my first year of Computer Science at Waterloo in September 2017. I am truly passionate about math and programming. Within this area, I am fascinated by fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, quantitative finance, web development and much more.

Otherwise, I also take an interest in physics, economics and building robots, a trait I acquired from spending countless hours on the FIRST Robotics team in my school (Team 6378 LYNX). Through lots of hard work and dedication, we made it to the FIRST World Championship in St. Louis (as a rookie team!); undoubtedly one of the best experiences I have ever had.

My hobbies are also quite diverse. In particular, you can convince me to play a game of chess at literally any time in the day. Also, I have a passion for both watching and playing cricket and going running early in the morning, when everything is peaceful and quiet. In addition, I enjoy travelling and strumming the guitar on occasion. You can check out some of the SoundCloud remixes and guitar covers that I've done on my WordPress website!
