About Me

Hey there! My name is Roshan Munjal, and I'll be going into my second year of Computer Science at Waterloo. I really enjoy about problem solving - in mathematics, in programming and in everything else. I like working with data to make insightful decisions, alongside the technical challenges of processing large amounts of data.

Otherwise, I also take an interest in robotics, a trait I acquired from spending countless hours on the FIRST Robotics team in my school (Team 6378 LYNX). Through lots of hard work and dedication, we made it to the FIRST World Championship in St. Louis (as a rookie team!); undoubtedly one of the best experiences I have ever had.

My hobbies are also quite diverse. In particular, you can convince me to play a game of chess at any time in the day. In addition, I enjoy travelling and strumming the guitar on occasion. You can find my SoundCloud remixes and guitar covers on my WordPress website!
